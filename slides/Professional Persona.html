The Professional Persona

How to present yourself to industry
📄 The Resume
  • The "Justify It" Rule
    Don't list a skill (e.g., "Optimization") if you can't explain the math behind it on a whiteboard.
  • Projects > Certificates
    Recruiters care about what you have built, not just which videos you have watched.
🌐 LinkedIn & Brand
  • Your Modern CV
    It's a living document. Post your academic achievements, hackathon wins, and project GitHub links.
  • Active Networking
    Don't just add connections. Engage with Alumni and industry leaders. Be visible.
🤖 Smart AI Usage
  • Co-Pilot, Not Autopilot
    Use ChatGPT to explain errors or suggest optimizations, not to write code from scratch.
  • The "Why" Test
    If AI writes it, you must be able to debug it. Interviews test your logic, not your syntax.
