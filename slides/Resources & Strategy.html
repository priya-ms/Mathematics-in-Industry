Experience Prerequisites

Why some roles require a "Test Drive" first
Internship is Essential
AI Architect
You need to learn how to use expensive company supercomputers. This hardware isn't available at home or in most classrooms.
The Strategist
Mistakes here cost real money. Companies want to see that you can handle high-pressure trading environments without panicking.
The Optimizer
School math is "perfect," but real-world data is messy. You need to see how a real factory or warehouse actually operates to fix it.
Major Competitive Benefit
The Predictor
Knowing the math is one thing, but knowing how a business works (like Retail or Medicine) makes your predictions actually useful.
The Simulator
You can learn the software alone, but working in a team to build a massive virtual world is a skill that makes you hireable instantly.
The Guardian
It proves you are trustworthy. An internship shows you can protect sensitive data without accidentally breaking the company’s systems.
